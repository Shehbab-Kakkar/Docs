Google Cloud provides powerful logging and monitoring capabilities via dedicated services. Here are their official, present-day names:

---

## 1. **Cloud Logging**

- **Present Name:**  
  **Google Cloud Logging**  
  (sometimes referred to as just **Cloud Logging**)
- **Console Product Name:**  
  **Logging**
- **Service Namespace (APIs, IAM, etc.):**  
  `logging.googleapis.com`
- **Purpose:**  
  Collects, stores, and manages logs from Google Cloud resources and applications.

---

## 2. **Cloud Monitoring**

- **Present Name:**  
  **Google Cloud Monitoring**  
  (sometimes referred to as just **Cloud Monitoring**)
- **Console Product Name:**  
  **Monitoring**
- **Service Namespace (APIs, IAM, etc.):**  
  `monitoring.googleapis.com`
- **Purpose:**  
  Provides visibility into the performance, uptime, and overall health of cloud-powered applications.
