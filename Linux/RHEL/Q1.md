## ğŸ§  1. RHEL Boot Process (Deep)

**Answer:**

1. **BIOS/UEFI** initializes hardware.
2. Loads **GRUB2** from EFI or MBR.
3. GRUB loads **kernel + initramfs**.
4. **initramfs** mounts root FS temporarily.
5. `systemd` starts as PID 1.
6. systemd loads default **target**.

**Troubleshooting tip:**

* Use `rd.break` or `init=/bin/bash` for recovery.

vi /etc/fstab
